Our RPO Services

We offer customized recruitment process solutions to scale your business. Our robust RPO solutions effectively attract talent while minimizing hiring time (TAT) and costs.

One-Time Placements

Our one-time placement service sources candidates based on your job description. We collaborate closely to screen and select candidates who meet both technical and cultural requirements, ensuring a seamless hiring experience.

Permanent Staffing

We specialize in recruiting technical staff for deployment to your site. Staff remain on our payroll for minimum 12 months, giving you flexibility while we handle recruitment, payroll and administration.

Project-Based Placements

Ideal for short-term needs, we provide specialized technical staff for 3-6 month projects. Avoid long-term commitments while ensuring timely execution of your critical projects.

Volume Hiring

Our scalable solutions handle high-volume recruitment needs efficiently, maintaining quality while meeting aggressive hiring timelines for your business growth.

Executive Search

Specialized recruitment for leadership roles, combining market intelligence with discreet search methodologies to identify and attract top executive talent.

Talent Mapping

Comprehensive market analysis to identify and engage with potential candidates, creating pipelines for current and future hiring needs.

Our Recruitment Process

Requirement Analysis

We begin with in-depth discussions to understand your specific talent needs, company culture, and position requirements.

Talent Sourcing

Our team leverages multiple channels including databases, social networks, and referrals to identify qualified candidates.

Screening & Assessment

Rigorous screening processes including technical evaluations and behavioral assessments ensure candidate quality.

Interview Coordination

We manage the entire interview process, from scheduling to feedback collection, streamlining your hiring workflow.
